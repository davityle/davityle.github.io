Why Im Not Switching to Firefox

October 12, 2017

I’ve been very excited with the progress that Firefox has been making in recent times and have been contemplating making the switch from Chrome for a while. The release of Firefox 57 nudged me over the edge and I excitedly downloaded Firefox Developer Edition like I an imaginative child on Christmas morning. I took some time to customize it to my liking and then proceeded to use it as my primary browser over the weekend.
